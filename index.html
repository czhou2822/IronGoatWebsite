BULKHEAD

The World is Snow and Steel

Bulkhead is an action tower-defense game played in third-person. Playing as the Solider, build towers, freeze, and loot enemies in a struggle to survive and escape.

A Growing Storm

Bulkhead is an independent project, and is quickly expanding and changing.
